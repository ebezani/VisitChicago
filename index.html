Heart of America

Welcome to the Visit Chicago website! My name is Lilly B, and I am a student at CCC. I have created this website for my CSI 181 class final project. The website aims to help Chicago city visitors learn about the city's history, explore different activities, tours, and restaurants I would recommend from my experience. I also understand transportation could be challenging if you plan to drive around the city due to limited and expensive parking areas. I have dedicated a page for transportation suggestions you can utilize.

Below are some pictures I have taken to give you a snapshot of Chicago.

  • Ocean Sunset
  • Waterfall on the Beach

Chicago is a beautiful, busy, lively city with a lot to offer to visitors from all backgrounds and interests. Depending on the number of days you plan to visit, this site will help you identify popular activities and tours you can enjoy with your friends and family. I have also included restaurants I think are must try at least once before your departure.

  • Golden Gate Bridge
  • Rocky Shoreline
  • Waves Crashing

I enjoy traveling and exploring different cities and cultures all around the world. So far, I have visited 16 countries and 35 states in the US. When creating this website, I used my experience of visiting other cities and things I wished I knew ahead of time before visiting the city.

If you have the luxury to pick your travel month to Chicago, I would recommend late spring, early summer, and early fall due to milder weather. During May, June, September, and October in Chicago, you will also have the opportunity to experience the majority of the tours, activities, and parks that are typically closed over the winter.
