Fine Dining

Chicago Pizza

Chicago is known for its deep-dish pizza. If you like cheese, deep-dish pizza is a "must-try" food experience. Several pizza restaurants around downtown Chicago offer deep-dish pizza. However, Labriola is my favorite.
