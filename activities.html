Chi-Town Adventure

Visiting Chicago is going to be an adventure for you, your friends, and your family. Regardless of which time of the year you plan to visit Chicago, you will not run out of activities to do. I would recommend you plan to visit Chicago during May, June, September, or October if you have the option to plan out your vacation ahead of time.

Below you will find my top five activities per season that I think are worth trying. Please note that some of these activities might need reservation ahead of time.
