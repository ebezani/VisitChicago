My Kind of Town

Taking a guided tour is one of the best ways to learn about Chicago. There are many different types of Tours you can take year-round. However, Some of the Tours are only available between late April and early October. Please make sure you contact the touring company to ensure they will be running their services when you plan to visit Chicago.

The tours I have listed below are ones that I think will be worth your time if you are looking to learn the history of Chicago and get familiar with the different areas.

Hop-on Hop-off Open Top Bus Tour

The Chicago Hop-On and Hop-Off is a bus tour that takes you around the Chicago area. The bus tour has several stops. You can join the guided tour at any of the scheduled stops or hop off and wait for the next bus while you explore the area. The bus has a top deck, making it easy to view the City skyline and take great pictures.

Price Range: $35-$55 per 24 hours

Chicago River Architecture Cruise

Several excellent river cruises take you around the Chicago river identifying Chicago landmarks such as Tribune Tower, Wrigley Building, and Willis Tower. You will learn from the guided tour while enjoying the beautiful view.

Price Range: $50-$70

History, Culture and Architecture Walking Tour

If you enjoy walking and are up for the adventure of exploring Chicago, this is the tour for you. Most of the walk tours last between 60 minutes to 90 minutes. Before making the reservation for your tour, check the weather and select the best time of the day that works for you.

Price Range: $25-$60

Chicago Segway Tour

The Segway tour is my favort. If you have never used a Segway before, you have nothing to worry! The tour guide will take time at the beginning of the tour to go over all the rules and show you how to use a Segway. You will get to practice before you go out on the streets for the first time.

Price Range: $60-$75 for a 2-Hour Tour

Chicago's Greatest Hits Bicycle Tour

Bike Tours in Chicago are age-friendly. If you are traveling with kids, this would be a good option for you and your family. You have different options to select from, such as Bike Hike, Signature dishes bike tour, Lakefront neighborhood, and much more.

Price Range: $10-$60
